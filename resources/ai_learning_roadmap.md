# AI Learning Roadmap (After the Workshop)

You completed the RAG foundation. Here is a practical path forward.

## Level 1: You are here

**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**

Skills:

- document loading and chunking
- embeddings and vector search
- prompt grounding
- local prototyping

Build next:

- course assistant for your students
- policy Q&A bot for your office
- research paper assistant for your team

---

## Level 2: Better RAG

Topics:

- chunk size tuning
- metadata filters
- source citations
- reranking retrieved chunks
- handling "I don't know" gracefully

Practice project:

- upload 10 PDFs from one domain
- measure answer quality with 20 test questions

---

## Level 3: AI Agents

Difference:

- RAG answers questions
- Agents take actions (search, write, call tools)

Learn:

- function calling
- tool design
- agent loops
- LangGraph basics

Practice project:

- research assistant that searches, summarizes, and saves notes

---

## Level 4: Fine-tuning

When RAG is not enough:

- domain-specific language
- structured output at scale
- style/format control

Learn:

- dataset preparation
- evaluation
- when fine-tuning is worth the cost

---

## Level 5: Production AI

Move from prototype to reliable system:

- logging and monitoring
- access control
- privacy and governance
- cost management
- deployment on internal servers

For sensitive research data, prefer on-prem/local deployment.
